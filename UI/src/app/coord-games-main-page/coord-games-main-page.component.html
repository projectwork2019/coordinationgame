Coordination Game Experiment

In this experiment you play games where your goal is to coordinate with another player unknown to you.


The games are displayed as follows:

  1. First, select a point from the left.

  2. Then your partner chooses a point from the right without seeing your choice.

  3. You and your partner both win if you manage to choose points connected by a line - otherwise you both lose.

Please choose a point which you expect to be the best for winning. Only after playing all the games, you will see how successful you were.
